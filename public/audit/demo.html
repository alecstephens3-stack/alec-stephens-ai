The Constraint

"If you doubled your leads tomorrow, what would break first?"

Lead follow-up. Your team is already losing leads because response time averages 4+ hours.

Right now, every new inquiry sits in a shared inbox until someone manually reads it, categorizes the request, looks up relevant listings, and writes a personalized response. During peak periods, this process takes 4 to 6 hours. Industry data shows that responding within 5 minutes makes you 21x more likely to qualify the lead. Every hour of delay is costing you closed deals.
